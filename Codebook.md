#Codebook - Tidy Data

##Identifiers

**subjectID**:

A unique identifier for each of the 30 people in the sample

**activity**:

Type of activity performed. 6 levels:

1. Walking
2. Walking Upstairs
3. Walking Downstairs
4. Sitting
5. Standing
6. Laying
